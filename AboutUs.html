About US

The aim of SMILE FOUNDATION is to break the vicious cycle of poverty and social isolation and to restore hope for a better future. We believe that every person has the right to access resources and opportunities in order to live and develop with dignity and to become an active and contributing member of our society. We work for the poor and underprivileged children by providing them all the necessary items that are beneficial for every human being and to bring smiles on their faces.
